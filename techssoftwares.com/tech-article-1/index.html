AI and Edge Computing: The Future of Low-Latency Inference

Edge computing brings inference closer to devices, reducing latency and enabling real-time applications.

Why it matters

By pushing AI models to the edge (e.g., mobile, gateways, IoT), companies can run features without a round-trip to the cloud.

Examples include autonomous drones, AR headsets, and smart cameras. Developers optimize models using quantization, pruning and on-device accelerators.
